
Work on proving Serre-Duality, first for $\bP^n$, then hopefully for many closed subsets thereof.

The notion of a \emph{finitely adequate} $R$-module, which we hope will serve as a satsfactory replacement of the faulty category of coherent modules in our setting, is being developed in parallel in a different draft \href{https://felix-cherubini.de/adequate-modules.pdf}{here}.
